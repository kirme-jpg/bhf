Our Research, Your Impact

Did you know that 190 people die every day from heart attacks? Over a million people in the UK live with a heart rhythm problem. Thanks to BHF-funded research, those affected have a better chance at longer, healthier lives.

Each day, 13 babies are diagnosed with congenital heart disease. We’re funding crucial breakthroughs to protect these tiny hearts and give children a fighting chance.

Stroke causes around 38,000 deaths annually in the UK. Our research is focused on understanding this condition better and developing vital treatments to reduce its impact.

Our work has revolutionised treatments for heart attacks, heart rhythm disorders, and congenital heart defects - but heartbreak caused by these diseases still touches every community across the country.
